They are made from synthetic or fiberglass media and are highly efficient at capturing fine particles like dust, pollen, and other airborne contaminants.
  • High filtration efficiency.
  • Large surface area.
  • Low pressure drop.

Multi-Pocket Bag Filters are constructed from high quality synthetic media pockets attached to an interlocked support 22mm header frame. The header slots into a 50/100mm holding frame easily. Pre-filters are usually used to preserve the life of the bag. It is used to remove fine particles in humid, high air flow and heavy dust loading conditions. Applications: Multi-Pocket bags are ideal for pre-filters to protect HEPA filters or as a final filter. Wide range of applications include: Hospitals, Cleanrooms, Industrial, Food Industry and Shopping Centre's etc.
